Classes

Mat Pilates

Mat Pilates focuses on strength, flexibility, and control using only your body and the mat. Our classes welcome all levels, with expert guidance to help you progress safely and confidently.

Reformer Pilates

Reformer Pilates combines resistance and support from the reformer machine to create a dynamic, full-body workout. It’s incredibly versatile, offering everything from gentle rehabilitation to challenging strength training and always engaging and fun. Our classes welcome all levels, with expert instructors helping you get the most out of every session.
